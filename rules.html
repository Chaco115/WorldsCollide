Wasteland Rift RP Server

Rules, Systems & Guidelines

General Roleplay Factions Territory Combat Rift System Custom Factions Staff Rules Evidence

General Rules

No RDM, no NLR abuse, no metagaming, no powergaming.

Respect all players and staff.

Stay in character unless in OOC areas.

Roleplay Rules

All actions must be realistically roleplayed.

Fear RP must be followed when outmatched or disarmed.

No unrealistic behavior that breaks immersion.

Faction Rules

Each faction must maintain rank structure.

Follow leadership orders unless they break server rules.

Faction switching requires approval.

Territory & Bases

Gas stations and police stations are contested zones.

Bases must be realistic and grounded in RP.

Raiding requires valid RP reason and escalation.

Combat Rules

Clear initiation is required before combat.

No combat logging.

Downed players are incapacitated unless revived.

Rift System

Players spawn through a random rift event.

New arrivals must RP confusion/disorientation.

Factions may recruit or intercept arrivals.

Custom Factions

Must be staff approved.

No overpowered or lore-breaking factions.

Must have clear RP purpose and structure.

Staff Rules & Abuse

Staff must remain neutral and fair at all times.

No using commands for personal gain or RP advantage.

Abuse of powers results in removal or punishment.

All actions must be documented when possible.

Evidence Requirements

Video Required: NLR, RDM, stealing, raiding issues, combat logging.

Video or Strong Evidence: Powergaming, metagaming, kidnapping disputes.

Chat Logs Accepted: Hate speech, harassment, OOC abuse.

Staff may request additional evidence at any time.
