Server Rules

Follow these rules to keep Worlds Collide fair, fun, and balanced for everyone.

No Exploiting

Using glitches, hacks, or unintended mechanics to gain advantage is strictly forbidden.

HIGH SEVERITY

No Toxicity

Harassment, hate speech, or targeted insults toward players or staff will not be tolerated.

HIGH SEVERITY

No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Do not attack or kill players without valid roleplay reasoning or engagement.

MEDIUM SEVERITY

No New Life Rule (NLR) Breaking

If you die, you cannot return to the same situation or remember prior events.

MEDIUM SEVERITY

Respect Staff Decisions

Staff have final say in disputes. Arguing excessively or refusing moderation may lead to punishment.

HIGH SEVERITY

No Stealing / Duping

Any form of item duplication or unauthorized taking of player assets is prohibited.

HIGH SEVERITY

Zero Tolerance: Child Exploitation

Any form of sexual exploitation, grooming, or inappropriate conduct involving minors is strictly prohibited. This includes any behavior, messaging, or roleplay that references or implies such actions.

Violations will result in an immediate and permanent ban from the server, and may be reported to appropriate legal authorities or platforms depending on the severity of the situation.

ZERO TOLERANCE
